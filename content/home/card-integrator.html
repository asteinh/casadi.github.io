Initial value problems in ordinary or differential-algebraic equations (ODE/DAE) can be calculated using explicit or implicit Runge-Kutta methods or interfaces to IDAS/CVODES from the SUNDIALS suite.

Problem class: $$ \begin{aligned} \dot{x} &= f_{\text{ode}}(t,x,z,p), \qquad x(0) = x_0 \\ 0 &= f_{\text{alg}}(t,x,z,p) \end{aligned} $$
