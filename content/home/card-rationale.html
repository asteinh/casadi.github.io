What CasADi offers...

  • Matrix-valued expression graphs.
    • Efficient sparse Jacobians through algorithmic differentiation on expressions.
    • Embed differentiable rootfinders, integrators, lookup tables, custom codes, ... in your expressions.
    • Generate stand-alone C code from expressions.
  • Enjoy interfaces to nonlinear programming (NLP) or quadratic programming (QP) codes.
